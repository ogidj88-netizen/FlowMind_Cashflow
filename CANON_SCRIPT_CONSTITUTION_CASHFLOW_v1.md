# SCRIPT_CONSTITUTION_CASHFLOW_v1

STATUS  
CANONICAL — DO NOT MODIFY WITHOUT VERSION UPDATE  

---

## SCOPE

Applies to:

- Money Mistakes
- Invisible Costs
- Cashflow Mode long-form (7–15 min, optimal 9–11)

---

## I. CORE PRINCIPLE

Every scene must:

- Show financial loss  
- Explain loss mechanism  
- Or show how to stop loss  

No function → no scene.

---

## II. HOOK (≤15s)

Must include:

- Measurable loss (money or time converted to money)  
- Clear mechanism  
- No greeting  
- No intro fluff  

Forbidden:

- “Hi guys”
- “Welcome back”
- Empty shock statements
- Fear manipulation
- Unrealistic promises

---

## III. STRUCTURE (MANDATORY)

1. Hook  
2. Context + 1 verified number  
3. Mechanism  
4. Peak (strongest financial impact)  
5. Second impact  
6. Practical control (no guarantees)  
7. Bridge to next video  

---

## IV. RHYTHM

- ≤20 words per sentence  
- Emotional impulse every 30–60 sec  
- No paragraphs >4 lines  

---

## V. HUMOR LAYER (REQUIRED)

Must include:

- 1 cultural reference  
- 1 ironic moment  
- 1 unexpected comparison  

Style inspiration:

- Dry Clarkson-style irony  
- Reynolds self-aware tone  
- American stand-up rhythm  

Forbidden:

- Political propaganda  
- Audience humiliation  
- Toxic sarcasm  

---

## VI. YMYL SAFETY GATE

Forbidden:

- Personalized financial advice  
- Guarantees  
- Legal/Tax manipulation tips  
- Risky financial encouragement  

Must:

- Neutral tone  
- Generalized education  
- Verified sources  

---

## VII. SOURCES WHITELIST

Allowed:

- BLS  
- Federal Reserve  
- SEC  
- IRS (general stats only)  
- CFPB  
- FTC  
- OECD  
- World Bank  
- IMF  
- Pew Research  
- Gallup  
- McKinsey / Deloitte / PwC reports  
- Bloomberg  
- Reuters  
- WSJ  
- Financial Times  

Rules:

- Min 1 source  
- Max 3 sources  
- Direct links in description  
- No “studies show” without citation  

---

## VIII. AI ANTI-ROBOT FILTER

Script FAIL if:

- Repetitive sentence patterns  
- Abstract claims without example  
- Generic filler transitions  
- No live example  

Must include:

- Example  
- Micro story  
- Rhetorical question (1–2 max)  

---

## IX. FINAL RULE

We sell financial clarity, not dreams.

---

END OF CONSTITUTION
